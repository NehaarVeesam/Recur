Statement:
Given two numbers, hour and minutes, return the smaller angle (in degrees) formed between the hour and the minute hand.

Answers within 10-5 of the actual value will be accepted as correct.

Approach: Math Concept
Time Complexity: O(1)
Space Complexity: O(1)
1. Clock has complete 360 degrees , and we have 12 hour hand so between each we have 360/12 -> 30 degrees
2. Minute hand completes 1 full revolution (circle -> 360 deg) in 60 minutes , therefore in 1 minute the minute hand moves 6 degrees
3. Similarly the hour hand completes full 1 revolution in 12 hours which is 720 minutes , therefore in 1 hour the hour hand moves 30 degrees
4. So in 60 minutes the hour hand moves 30 degrees , in 1 minute the hour hand moves 0.5 degrees 
Therefore hour angle -> 30* (hour value) + 0.5 * (minute value)
And minute angle -> 6*(minute value)

Difference between hour and minute hand is | 30* (hour value) + 0.5 * (minute value) - 6*(minute value)|

Learning:
1. Maths concept of hour angle and minute angle

Mistakes:
While finding out the smallest angle , you need to check 360 degrees - also to get smallest value

Code:
class Solution:
    def angleClock(self, h: int, m: int) -> float:
        return min(abs(30*(h%12) + 0.5*m - 6*m), 360 - abs(30*(h%12) + 0.5*m - 6*m))
